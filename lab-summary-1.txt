Lab Summary 1

Tool used:
Python + psutil + Sysinternals

What I checked:
I collected a snapshot of selected browser and launcher processes and recorded their parent processes.

What I found:
Most msedge.exe child processes were spawned by a main msedge.exe process.
The main msedge.exe process was spawned by explorer.exe.
No unusual parent-child relationships were found.
One steam.exe process had an unknown parent because the parent could not be resolved at collection time.

Why it matters:
Parent-child process relationships help show whether process behavior looks normal or suspicious.
